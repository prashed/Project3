Why You Should Care:

If you’re not concerned about online privacy you need to look no further than China to see the reality of the Internet without privacy. The Chinese government Tightly cooperates With the corporations including Google To build tools that track everybody’s online activities. The government then uses the data to give people social score. People have no control over their social score often results in individuals being banned from travelling, shopping, or engaging in social activities. It’s only a matter of short time before these tools start being implemented in the West too. They’re after all partly developed by Western corporations. Before it happens, you can save yourself from the future if you can learn how to be anonymous on the web.
