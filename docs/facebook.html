Facebook Dilemma

Facebook

Zuckerberg’s challenge was to show investors and advertisers The profit that could be made from Facebook’s most valuable asset, the personal data it had on its users. Great as Zuckerberg was, he had very little experience in building a big advertising business. That would be the job of Zuckerberg‘s deputy, Sheryl Sandberg who’d done the same for Google. The business model we see today was created by Sheryl Sandberg and the team she built. Facebook many of whom had been with her when she’d done the same for Google. Sandberg and Zuckerberg had been downplaying the extent of the personal data Facebook was collecting and emphasising user’s privacy, but internally Sanberg would soon lead Facebook in a different direction.
